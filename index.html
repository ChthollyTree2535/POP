THE SURVEY

POP is a piggyback pulsar survey conducted by the FAST science group. It searches drift-scan observations for pulsars, intermittent emitters and fast radio bursts while making the resulting catalogue useful to the wider pulsar community.

We use single-pulse searches for long-period pulsars, RRATs and FRBs; FFT searches for millisecond pulsars and binary pulsars; and FFA searches for intermediate- and long-period pulsars.
